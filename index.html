ABOUT


A Hack & Win @ Krishna is an event of 12 hours where people come together to solve problems. This is a problem solving event.(It does not have to involve technology.) with three days workshops (March 14th,16th and 18th).

The hardest thing about running a successful hackathon is being welcoming to newcomers and helping them get involved in an activity.

Here are the goals we keep in mind:
• Strengthen the community that the hackathon is for.
• Be welcoming to newcomers to the community.
• Provide an opportunity for participants to learn something new.
• Provide a space and a time for participants to make headway on problems they are interested in.

Real life problems are hard! Think of the hackathon as a pit-stop on a long journey to solve problems or as a training session to prepare participants for solving problems.
